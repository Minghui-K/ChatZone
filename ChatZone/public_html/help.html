Me

To fill your information, you may go to click -Me- button. Home

On the home page, there are three columns right there. When you just login, the 4th column will be a welcome message for you. On the 1st column, there are three different lists for you to choose. Added Friend is the friends you added and you may click there name to view there information on the 4th column, and click chat button on there then you can chat with your friend on the 3rd column. New Friend is the people who want to add you and sent you a request to be friend, you may also view the information of them on the 4th column and choose accept to be friend with him/her. The last choice is the strangers that not in the privious two, you may view the information and decide if you want to add them. Otherwise, for each list, you can use search bar to search the people by their username.
